# Quo. — Support

Quo. is a minimal daily quote app for iOS.

## Common questions

**My widget shows an old quote.**
Open the app once so it can refresh, or remove and re-add the widget. After an app update, restarting your iPhone clears the widget cache.

**When does my quote change?**
At exact clock boundaries, local time: midnight (daily), noon and midnight (12-hour), Sunday midnight (weekly), the first of the month at midnight (monthly). The Constant setting never changes the quote.

**The widget changes quotes by itself — is that normal?**
Yes. Quo. schedules upcoming quotes with iOS so the widget rotates on time even when the app is closed.
